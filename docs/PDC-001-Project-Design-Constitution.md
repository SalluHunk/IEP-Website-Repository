## Article I — Executive Summary

Industrial Energy Pioneers (IEP) is an engineering-led consultancy, established in 2022 by three senior professionals from engineering, energy-systems, and finance backgrounds, that helps energy-intensive industrial and commercial organisations reduce operating costs and waste, improve margins, and fund and deliver commercially viable efficiency and decarbonisation projects.

This website exists to modernise IEP's digital presence and to serve as the primary channel through which IEP converts engineering and financial credibility into qualified commercial enquiries. It shall strengthen IEP's market positioning, communicate its services and expertise clearly, evidence measurable client outcomes, and establish a durable foundation for IEP's continued growth.

A visitor shall immediately understand that IEP is not a generic sustainability consultancy: it is a senior-led, engineering-and-finance-credible partner that delivers measurable, commercially justified outcomes — reduced costs, funded projects, delivered results — rather than reports, studies, or unproven recommendations.

**Constitutional Statement:** This website exists to convert IEP's engineering and financial credibility into qualified commercial enquiries, by demonstrating — through evidenced, outcome-led communication — that IEP delivers commercially viable results where generic consultancies deliver only studies.

## Article II — Project Vision

*Derived from: Project Proposal, Client Questionnaire, IEP Brochure.*

The website's role for the business is to function as IEP's primary trust-building and enquiry-generation channel — the Commercial Proposal's own framing describes it as a "Homepage Experience Blueprint" — carrying at scale the credibility currently carried by direct introduction and personal reputation.

Visitors shall perceive IEP as a senior-led, commercially disciplined engineering and sustainability partner — not a junior-staffed generic consultancy — whose recommendations are grounded in practical engineering judgement, financial rigour, and delivered outcomes rather than aspiration.

The website shall be built on a scalable technical foundation capable of supporting future content and functional growth, on the agreed WordPress CMS platform.

⚠️ **Ambiguity flagged, not resolved.** The Project Proposal and Commercial Proposal each describe a phased future roadmap (CRM integration, marketing automation, AI-powered tools, client dashboards, energy-assessment calculators) as natural next-phase opportunities. However, the Client Questionnaire's own Future Roadmap section was answered "Nothing decided as of now." This Article therefore establishes only that the website's technical foundation must be scalable — it does not establish which, if any, of the vendor-proposed future features are approved client direction. Any future-phase feature work requires a fresh Tier 1 (explicit client approval) source before it can be treated as verified.

## Article IV — Company Positioning

### Who IEP Is

The website shall position Industrial Energy Pioneers as:

- **An engineering-led consultancy**, not a marketing-led one — credibility flows from engineering and financial expertise, not from communications capability. *(Statement of Work; Commercial Proposal, Strategic Positioning; Client Questionnaire leadership profiles.)*
- **Commercially focused** — every recommendation is framed by its business and financial outcome, not only its technical or environmental merit. *(Commercial Proposal, Strategic Positioning core themes; IEP Brochure funding-capability positioning.)*
- **Led by senior specialists** — the organisation's credibility rests on named, individually credentialed leaders, not an anonymous or junior delivery team. *(Client Questionnaire §3, Leadership Team Profiles — verified biographies for Andy Holgate, Dr. Abhishek Asthana, Tim Griffiths.)*
- **Evidence-driven** — claims are supported by delivered, measurable outcomes rather than projected or aspirational figures. *(Commercial Proposal, Project Objectives: "Highlight measurable client outcomes"; Statement of Work §2.4, Case Studies page purpose.)*
- **A practical implementation partner**, not only an advisory one — recommendations are carried through to delivery. *(IEP Brochure / content-briefing positioning; Statement of Work methodology framing.)*
- **Outcome-oriented** — business outcomes and operational performance lead the narrative, ahead of process or technology description. *(Commercial Proposal, Strategic Positioning: core themes stated as an alternative to "simply listing services.")*
- **Financially credible** — the organisation holds genuine investment, funding, and financial-structuring capability, not only engineering capability. *(Client Questionnaire §3 — Tim Griffiths' project-finance background; Dr. Asthana's grant-funding track record; IEP Brochure funding positioning.)*
- **A long-term partner** — the relationship model extends beyond a single transaction. *(Commercial Proposal, Post-Launch Support / AMC provisions; closing-note partnership framing.)* Lower confidence than the statements above: evidenced by the engagement's support structure rather than a direct positioning statement in the source material.

### Who IEP Is Not

The website shall not communicate Industrial Energy Pioneers as:

- **A generic sustainability consultancy**, whose recommendations are justified primarily by environmental or ESG metrics. *(IEP Brochure / content-briefing internal differentiation content: recommendations must be technically sound and commercially justified, not chosen because they improve sustainability metrics or reporting optics.)*
- **An ESG marketing or reporting agency.** *(Same source — stated focus is delivery, not documentation that goes unused.)*
- **A junior engineering practice.** *(Inverse of the Senior Specialists positioning above; Client Questionnaire leadership profiles.)*
- **A technology or software vendor.** *(Reasoned inference: the Commercial Proposal's Exclusions place SaaS and AI product development outside this engagement's scope, consistent with an engineering/consultancy identity — not a direct positioning statement in the source evidence.)*
- **An academic or research-only organisation**, notwithstanding the academic credentials held by its leadership. *(IEP Brochure / content-briefing — explicit delivery-over-research framing; Client Questionnaire leadership profiles combine academic credentials with commercial delivery track records.)*
- **A report-producing consultancy** whose output is documentation rather than delivered change. *(IEP Brochure / content-briefing — explicit outcomes-over-activity framing.)*

**Constitutional Positioning Statement:** Industrial Energy Pioneers shall be positioned as a senior-led, commercially and financially credible engineering consultancy that delivers — not merely recommends — measurable operational and financial outcomes for energy-intensive industrial and commercial organisations.

## Article V — Audience Constitution

### Primary Audience

Senior operational and technical decision-makers — Operations Directors, Engineering Directors, Plant/Site Managers — at energy-intensive industrial and commercial organisations within IEP's verified sector focus: FMCG manufacturing, construction materials, food & beverage, pharmaceuticals, packaging, and multisite operators. *(IEP Brochure — "Who We Help" sector list.)*

### Secondary Audience

Financial and executive decision-makers — Finance Directors, CFOs, Managing Directors — whose approval determines whether an identified opportunity becomes a funded, delivered project. *(IEP Brochure / content-briefing — "Board-ready investment analyses" as a stated capability; funding-capability positioning.)* This audience is secondary in sequence, not in importance: it typically enters the decision process after a technical case has been identified, and its concerns are distinct from the Primary Audience's operational concerns.

### Decision Makers

Because the offer requires both technical sign-off and financial sign-off, the website shall assume dual decision-making — a technical sponsor who identifies the opportunity and a financial/executive approver who authorises it — rather than a single buyer persona. *(Derived from IEP Brochure funding-capability positioning and the Commercial Proposal's executive-focused messaging framing; not a directly stated persona in the verified source material.)*

### Business Drivers

- Rising or volatile energy and utilities costs.
- Pressure to reduce operating costs and protect margin.
- Sustainability and decarbonisation obligations that must be commercially justified, not pursued for their own sake.

*(IEP Brochure — "What We Do"; Commercial Proposal, Business Value Focus.)*

### Commercial Motivations

- Securing capital investment approval for efficiency and decarbonisation projects.
- Accessing grant funding to reduce the capital burden of those projects.
- Converting identified opportunities into delivered, verifiable results rather than shelved recommendations.

*(IEP Brochure funding-capability and "Board-ready investment analyses" content; Commercial Proposal, Business Value Focus.)*

### Pain Points

- Uncertainty about whether a proposed improvement is commercially justified.
- Difficulty securing internal or Board approval for capital investment.
- Prior experience of consultancy engagements that produced reports rather than delivered outcomes.

*(IEP Brochure / content-briefing differentiation positioning; Commercial Proposal, Strategic Positioning.)*

### Decision Criteria

- Demonstrated, measurable outcomes from comparable engagements (case studies).
- Seniority and credibility of the team proposing the work.
- Evidence of funding and financial capability, not only technical capability.

*(Client Questionnaire §1.1 / §3; Commercial Proposal, Project Objectives.)*

### Visitor Questions

Derived from the evidence above, a visitor arriving at the website is likely to be asking:

- Can this organisation actually deliver, or only advise? *(IEP Brochure / content-briefing — delivery-over-reports positioning.)*
- Can they help justify this investment internally? *(Funding-capability positioning; "Board-ready investment analyses.")*
- Can they help secure funding for this? *(Same.)*
- Is this team senior enough, and credible enough, to trust with this? *(Client Questionnaire leadership profiles.)*
- Will their recommendations work within our existing operations, not just in theory? *(IEP Brochure / content-briefing — practical, operational-reality framing.)*
- Have they done this before, in a comparable business? *(Commercial Proposal, "Highlight measurable client outcomes"; Statement of Work, Case Studies page purpose.)*

**Constitutional Audience Statement:** This website exists for two audiences who must be convinced together — the senior operational or technical sponsor who identifies the opportunity, and the financial or executive approver who authorises it — and every page shall address both the operational credibility and the financial credibility of IEP's offer.

## Article VI — Design Constitution

### Executive Design Philosophy

The website's visual identity shall be predominantly that of a premium, executive consulting practice, with selective engineering-specialist visual signals used to reinforce technical credibility rather than to dominate the aesthetic. *(Commercial Proposal, Branding & Visual Identity — the engagement's own visual-direction framing balances a consulting-firm aesthetic against an industrial-engineering-specialist aesthetic, in favour of the former.)* This balance reflects the Company Positioning established in Article IV: commercially credible first, technically credible throughout.

### Colour Philosophy

- IEP's existing brand colours shall be preserved, not redesigned.
- White and light backgrounds shall remain the dominant visual language across the site.
- Dark graphite sections may be used selectively, to create emphasis and narrative contrast at specific moments — not as a default treatment.
- Green shall remain the primary accent colour.

⚠️ **Ambiguity resolved, not silently.** The Statement of Work's original Branding & Visual Identity section described a Teal/Coral working palette as a starting concept-development direction. The Client Questionnaire's Design Validation section, answered by the client, instead confirmed a "Green Brand Accent Colour" and a "Light Grey Background Theme." The principles above reflect the latter, client-confirmed direction, as relayed and confirmed via approved client review (Mission PDC-001-B, 2026-07-05). This client review has not yet been catalogued as a standalone Client Review Notes source in KNOWLEDGE-SOURCES-001 — registering it is recommended as a follow-up per the Knowledge Maintenance Workflow.

### Typography Philosophy

Typography shall create hierarchy through scale, not decoration. Large section headings are preferred over ornamentation or a wide variety of typographic treatments. *(Constitutional principle relayed via approved client review, Mission PDC-001-B.)*

### Photography Philosophy

Photography shall be authentic and industrial in character. Generic corporate stock photography shall be avoided. *(Approved client review, Mission PDC-001-B; consistent with the content-briefing document's imagery guidance — see Industrial Imagery Philosophy below.)*

### Industrial Imagery Philosophy

Priority imagery shall be drawn from genuine industrial and engineering subject matter: energy systems (boilers, steam systems, heat exchangers, CHP, thermal storage), process engineering (manufacturing lines, kilns, furnaces, process control rooms), utilities (water/wastewater treatment, compressed air, cooling systems), resource recovery (anaerobic digestion, biomass, waste-to-energy), and engineering visualisation (CFD models, thermal imaging, engineering drawings, industrial dashboards).

Imagery shall explicitly avoid: wind turbines, generic solar farms, hands holding globes, and generic ESG-style stock imagery. *(Content-briefing document, Industrial Image Library Brief.)*

Diagrams and engineering visuals are encouraged wherever they improve a visitor's understanding of a technical or process concept. *(Approved client review, Mission PDC-001-B.)*

### Motion Philosophy

Motion shall support comprehension — drawing attention to a result, a process step, or a change in state — and shall not be used for entertainment or decorative effect. *(Approved client review, Mission PDC-001-B.)*

### User Experience Philosophy

The user experience shall feel calm, executive, and trustworthy throughout. Interfaces shall avoid overly dark treatments as a default, and shall avoid futuristic or "cyber" aesthetics inconsistent with the Executive Design Philosophy above. *(Approved client review, Mission PDC-001-B.)*

### Accessibility Philosophy

Body text shall maintain strong contrast against its background at all times; pale or low-contrast body text shall be avoided. Text and background combinations shall meet, at minimum, WCAG AA contrast compliance. *(Content-briefing document, Text Contrast & Accessibility guidance.)*

**Constitutional Design Statement:** The website's design shall communicate calm, restrained, executive credibility — built on IEP's existing brand colours and a predominantly light visual language — using authentic industrial imagery and purposeful motion to reinforce comprehension and trust, never to entertain or to imitate a generic technology aesthetic.

## Article VII — Content Constitution

### Communication Philosophy

The website shall communicate according to the following principles:

- **Executive-first communication.** *(CRN-001, Content Direction; Commercial Proposal, Strategic Positioning — executive-focused messaging framing.)*
- **Commercial outcomes before engineering explanation.** *(CRN-001, Content Direction: "Commercial outcomes before technical detail"; Commercial Proposal, Strategic Positioning core themes.)*
- **Plain UK English.** *(CRN-001, Content Direction; content-briefing document, UK English Standards; Client Questionnaire §10, Design Validation: "UK English Throughout," confirmed.)*
- **Evidence before opinion.** *(Commercial Proposal, Project Objectives: "Highlight measurable client outcomes"; Statement of Work §2.4, Case Studies page purpose: "evidence-based credibility.")*
- **Authenticity before persuasion.** *(IEP Brochure / content-briefing document — internal differentiation content: recommendations must be technically sound and commercially justified, not chosen for how they present.)*
- **Confidence without exaggeration.** *(Approved client review direction, relayed via this mission (PDC-001 Mission C, 2026-07-05) and consistent with CRN-001's Content Direction; not independently stated in the Proposal or Brochure — recorded here on the strength of relayed client review alone.)*
- **Practical engineering rather than theory.** *(IEP Brochure / content-briefing document — delivery-over-research framing; consistent with Article IV, "A practical implementation partner.")*
- **Benefits before technical features.** *(Same grounding as "Commercial outcomes before engineering explanation" above — a restatement at the feature/benefit level of the same principle.)*
- **Short, scannable content.** *(CRN-001, UX: "Easy to scan," "High readability.")*
- **Consistent terminology.** *(Content-briefing document, UK English Standards — the existence of a defined terminology standard, e.g. "Optimise" not "Optimize," evidences a broader consistent-terminology requirement.)*

### Writing Principles

The website shall:
- communicate with clarity;
- minimise unnecessary jargon;
- explain technical capability through business value;
- avoid promotional exaggeration;
- avoid unsupported claims;
- use measurable outcomes where available.

*(Approved client review direction, relayed via this mission; consistent with Article III's evidence-traceability discipline and CRN-001's Content Direction.)*

### Messaging Hierarchy

Content shall be ordered:

Business Outcome → Commercial Value → Engineering Capability → Technical Detail

This hierarchy is not a new invention — it is the constitutional synthesis of two already-materialized Articles: Article III's Business Objective 5 ("Present commercial outcomes before technical capability") and Article IV's Company Positioning ("Outcome-oriented... business outcomes and operational performance lead the narrative, ahead of process or technology description"). *(Commercial Proposal, Strategic Positioning core themes.)*

**Content Principle:** All website content shall lead with commercial outcome and business relevance, support that claim with evidence, and reserve technical and engineering depth for readers who seek it — never the reverse.

## Article VIII — Homepage Narrative Constitution

This Article defines the visitor's homepage journey — story, not layout or design. It is the governing narrative sequence for the homepage; it does not prescribe how that narrative is visually expressed.

### Narrative Sequence

**1. Executive Trust**
- Purpose: Immediate credibility.
- Primary Message: IEP is a senior-led, credible engineering and financial partner. *(Article IV, Company Positioning; Statement of Work §2.4, Home page purpose: "Primary brand statement, hero narrative, value proposition, and key CTAs.")*
- Visitor Question: Can I trust these people?
- Desired Response: Confidence.
- Transition Logic: Why should I care?

**2. Commercial Challenge**
- Purpose: Frame the commercial problem.
- Primary Message: Energy and operational inefficiency is a commercial, not only an environmental, problem. *(Commercial Proposal, Business Value Focus; IEP Brochure, "What We Do.")*
- Visitor Question: Is this relevant to my business?
- Desired Response: Recognition.
- Transition Logic: Who does this affect?

**3. Who We Help**
- Purpose: Define the audience.
- Primary Message: IEP works with energy-intensive industrial and commercial organisations across specific, named sectors. *(Statement of Work §2.4, Industries page purpose: "Sector focus areas and industrial expertise display"; IEP Brochure sector list; Article V, Primary Audience.)*
- Visitor Question: Is this company built for organisations like mine?
- Desired Response: Relevance.
- Transition Logic: Why choose IEP?

**4. Why IEP**
- Purpose: Differentiate.
- Primary Message: IEP is senior-led, commercially disciplined, and delivery-focused — not a generic consultancy. *(Article IV, Who IEP Is / Is Not.)*
- Visitor Question: Why these consultants?
- Desired Response: Preference.
- Transition Logic: What exactly do they do?

**5. Services**
- Purpose: Present capability.
- Primary Message: IEP's service pillars map directly to the commercial challenge already established. *(Statement of Work §2.4, Services page purpose: "Service pillars, methodology, and structured capability presentation.")*
- Visitor Question: Can they solve my problem?
- Desired Response: Confidence.
- Transition Logic: How do they work?

**6. Methodology**
- Purpose: Reduce implementation risk.
- Primary Message: IEP follows a defined, evidence-first delivery process. This stage shall remain outcome-focused rather than a detailed technical walkthrough — the source material itself cautions against over-explaining method at the expense of outcome. *(Content-briefing document, "How We Do It" guidance: methodology should be shown, not exhaustively explained.)*
- Visitor Question: How will they deliver?
- Desired Response: Trust.
- Transition Logic: Can they prove capability?

**7. Technical Capability**
- Purpose: Emphasise engineering depth.
- Primary Message: IEP holds genuine in-house technical/simulation capability (e.g. CFD, FEA, process modelling). *(IEP Brochure / content-briefing document — technical capability content; Article VI, Industrial Imagery Philosophy — diagrams and engineering visuals encouraged here.)*
- Visitor Question: Can they validate recommendations?
- Desired Response: Credibility.
- Transition Logic: Who will I work with?

**8. Leadership**
- Purpose: Humanise expertise.
- Primary Message: Named, senior, individually credentialed leaders stand behind the claims made so far. *(Statement of Work §2.4, Leadership page purpose: "Team profiles, experience, and senior-led capability communication"; Client Questionnaire §3, Leadership Team Profiles.)*
- Visitor Question: Who are these people?
- Desired Response: Confidence.
- Transition Logic: I'm interested. What next?

**9. Call To Action**
- Purpose: Convert interest into engagement.
- Primary Message: A specific, low-friction next step is available. *(Statement of Work §2.4, Contact page purpose: "Smart enquiry capture with automated response and routing"; Article III, Business Objective 1: "Generate qualified commercial enquiries.")*
- Visitor Question: What should I do now?
- Desired Response: Take action.
- Transition Logic: End of homepage journey.

*Note on stage 7: this Article records the stage's purpose as "Emphasise engineering depth" — consistent with its Visitor Question ("Can they validate recommendations?") and Desired Response ("Credibility"), both of which require depth to be shown, not minimised.*

**Homepage Narrative Principle:** The nine-stage sequence above — Executive Trust, Commercial Challenge, Who We Help, Why IEP, Services, Methodology, Technical Capability, Leadership, Call To Action — is the constitutional homepage story. Every homepage revision, redesign, or CMS-driven regeneration of homepage content shall preserve this sequence and each stage's purpose; a revision may change presentation, but shall not reorder, omit, or repurpose a stage without a new Constitutional Amendment recorded in Article IX.

### Constitutional Amendments

1. **Homepage Narrative Confirmed.** The nine-stage sequence established in Article VIII is the governing homepage structure. It supersedes any homepage ordering that might otherwise be assumed from a source other than Article VIII / CRN-001. *(CRN-001, Homepage Narrative.)*
2. **Executive Messaging Strengthened.** Communication across the site shall be executive-first, per Article VII. This amends the Statement of Work's page-purpose descriptions (§2.4) toward a more explicitly senior/executive register than those descriptions alone specify. *(CRN-001, Content Direction.)*
3. **Typography Hierarchy Increased.** Heading sizes shall be significantly larger than a conventional corporate-website default, creating hierarchy through scale, per Article VI. This is an amendment beyond the Statement of Work's Branding & Visual Identity section, which does not address typographic scale. *(CRN-001, Typography.)*

## Article XII — Constitutional Governance

This Article governs the Constitution itself — how it is amended, versioned, and how it relates to the rest of the repository.

### Authority
- PDC-001 derives its authority from the Primary Knowledge Sources catalogued in KNOWLEDGE-SOURCES-001, not from itself.
- Primary Knowledge Sources remain authoritative over PDC-001 at every point of conflict. *(KNOWLEDGE-SOURCES-001 §4, Authority Hierarchy; §5, Repository Authority Principle.)*

### Amendments
- Client-approved decisions become constitutional amendments via a Client Review Notes source (e.g. CRN-001) and are recorded as Constitutional Amendments in Article IX.
- Every amendment shall be traceable to the Primary Source or approved client review decision that produced it. *(KNOWLEDGE-SOURCES-001 §7, Traceability Rules.)*

### Versioning
- PDC-001's version reflects its state of completion and shall not be marked frozen until all twelve Articles are complete and have passed Architecture Review.
- Future constitutional revisions shall preserve historical versions rather than overwrite them. *(KNOWLEDGE-SOURCES-001, Repository Principles — Incremental Evolution; §10, Repository Maturity.)*

### Traceability
- Every Article shall conclude with a Source References subsection identifying its Primary Sources and Related Repository Documents.
- No constitutional statement shall exist without a traceable source or an explicitly labelled inference. *(KNOWLEDGE-SOURCES-001, AI Behaviour — never invent requirements; distinguish recommendation from fact.)*

### Repository Precedence
- Repository documents inherit constitutional authority from PDC-001 once materialized; Specifications (`DL-001`, `DSS-001`, `CMS-001`, `SEO-001`) shall not introduce a principle inconsistent with this Constitution. *(KNOWLEDGE-SOURCES-001 §2, Repository Knowledge Model.)*
- Implementation documents and Claude missions shall never override the Constitution. Where an implementation instruction conflicts with a constitutional Article, the Article governs, and the conflict shall be flagged rather than silently resolved. *(KNOWLEDGE-SOURCES-001 §5, Conflict Resolution.)*

### Implementation Compliance
- Every implementation Mission shall be traceable to one or more Articles of this Constitution, directly or via an intervening Specification. *(KNOWLEDGE-SOURCES-001 §7, Traceability Rules.)*
- An implementation that cannot be traced to this Constitution shall not proceed without a new Constitutional Amendment.

### Future Evolution
- Major constitutional changes require Architecture Review before taking effect.
- The Constitution evolves incrementally, Article by Article and amendment by amendment — it is never rewritten wholesale. *(KNOWLEDGE-SOURCES-001 §10, Repository Maturity.)*

**Constitutional Governance Principle:** This Constitution governs the website; the Primary Knowledge Sources and approved client review govern this Constitution. No Article may be amended, and no future Article added, without a traceable source and a recorded Architecture Review.
