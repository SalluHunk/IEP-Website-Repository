## Executive Summary

Technology Innovation & R&D Support helps organisations develop, independently validate, and secure innovation-specific funding for emerging technologies. It combines structured technology assessment and pilot-programme development with independent performance validation and technology-readiness assessment, evaluating whether an emerging technology is genuinely ready for commercial deployment, not just technically promising.

Clients engage IEP here when developing or evaluating an emerging technology and needing independent, credible validation before committing capital, or when they need specialist support navigating innovation-specific funding routes such as Innovate UK and Horizon Europe — funding programmes that require different expertise from the general project funding covered under Funding, Procurement & Project Delivery.

The expected outcome is a validated, de-risked technology position: an independent technical review, a clear technology-readiness and commercial-viability assessment, and, where applicable, a successful innovation-funding application. This is a parallel track alongside IEP's core efficiency and decarbonisation lifecycle, not a stage within it.

## Business Challenges

- Emerging technologies that require independent, credible evaluation before capital is committed or commercial claims are made.
- Innovation-specific funding programmes — Innovate UK, Horizon Europe — that demand specialist application expertise distinct from general grant funding.
- Difficulty separating genuinely viable innovation from unproven technology claims without independent technical due diligence.

## Capabilities

**Research & Development Support**
- Technology assessment
- Pilot project and demonstrator programme development
- Innovate UK and Horizon Europe application support
- Technical due diligence

**Technology Validation**
- Independent performance validation
- Technology-readiness assessment
- Independent technical review
- Commercial-viability assessment and risk evaluation
- Investment analysis for innovation funding

## Typical Deliverables

- Technology assessments and independent technical reviews

- Innovate UK / Horizon Europe funding applications
- Technical due diligence reports
