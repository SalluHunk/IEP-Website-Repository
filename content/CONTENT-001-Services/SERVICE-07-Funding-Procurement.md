## Executive Summary

Funding, Procurement & Project Delivery converts an approved investment case into a funded, procured and implemented project. It covers government and grant funding applications, procurement process management and EPC contractor supervision, technical specification, and commissioning through to post-implementation performance verification.

Clients engage IEP here once a project has cleared Engineering Design, Feasibility & Investment Case and needs funding routes identified, procurement managed, and the physical work delivered and verified against its design intent.

The expected outcome is a delivered, working project with a reduced capital burden: grant funding secured against a proven track record of successful awards, procurement and contractor delivery managed end-to-end, and installed systems verified to perform as designed. This capability has secured grant funding on three of IEP's delivered engagements evidenced in this portfolio, converting an approved business case into a completed, funded outcome each time.

## Business Challenges

- Capital burden remaining a barrier to implementation even after a project has been justified.
- Grant application processes that require specialist knowledge most organisations do not hold in-house.
- Procurement and EPC contractor management representing execution risk distinct from the engineering design itself.
- No confirmation, without dedicated verification, that an installed system actually delivers the efficiency or decarbonisation objectives it was designed for.

## Capabilities

**Funding**
- Government and grant funding applications, for projects that have cleared Screening and Feasibility
- Evidence-pack preparation to support funding applications
- Track record of successful grant awards

**Procurement & Delivery**
- Procurement process management
- EPC contractor supervision
- Technical specification and project management

**Verification**
- Commissioning support
- Post-implementation performance verification against energy-efficiency and decarbonisation objectives

## Typical Deliverables

- Funding and grant applications, with supporting evidence packs
- Managed procurement processes, including EPC contractor supervision
- Technical specifications for implementation
- Commissioning support and performance verification reports

## Business Outcomes

- Grant funding secured and approved on three delivered engagements: a plastic packaging manufacturer, an aluminium recycling plant, and a specialist brick manufacturer
- Reduced capital burden and faster project implementation, reported as a direct consequence of this capability
