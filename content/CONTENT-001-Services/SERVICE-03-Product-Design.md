## Executive Summary

Product Design & Optimisation describes IEP's in-house simulation and modelling capability — Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD), Finite Element Analysis (FEA), AI process simulation, and industrial process modelling — rather than a service a client engages independently. Per the Service Portfolio Constitution, this capability supports the recommendations made under Energy, Utilities & Process Efficiency, Water, Wastewater & Circular Resource Management, Low-carbon & Resilient Energy Systems, and Engineering Design, Feasibility & Investment Case. No evidence describes it being offered, or engaged by a client, as a standalone service with its own business case.

Its purpose is validation: giving a client confidence that a recommended change will behave as predicted before capital is committed, rather than relying on judgement alone. Where this capability is presented publicly, it should read as a credibility signal embedded within the four services it supports, not as a fifth, independently purchasable offering — because no such offering currently exists in the evidence base.
